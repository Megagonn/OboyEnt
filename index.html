About us

Oboy Entertainment Founded: 2014 Founder: Christian Ekene Elue Country of origin: South Africa ocation: Johannesburg Official website: OboyEntertainment.com Oboy Entertainment is an South Africa record label founded by Christian Ekene Elue. Oboy Entertainment's president is Christian Ekene Elue. Oboy Entertainment operates as a music record label for singers. The Company's offers classical, pop, gospel and hip-hop music genres. We maintain a modern and well-run operation with an international network of affiliates and sub-publishers, providing outstanding personal service to all our clients. Above all, we are committed to the creative process, understanding and protecting the value of music in a rapidly evolving global environment.As a major music label, Oboy Entertainment's strategy first and foremost is to discover and bid for rising star artists and churn out hits.
