Biography

MMCandy “Kwaito Pooh” whose real name is MBALI MBATHA, An upcoming recording kwaito artist, actress, hair model, entrepreneur, & God fearing young lady who was born and bred in the township situated in the south west, eastcoast of Durban at “Umlazi” in Kzn province, THE KWAITO POOH was raised by her single mother “Sindy Mbatha” they are two from her mom and eight in total including her siblings from her Father. MMCandy is her grandfathers little angel.She studied in Durban where she completed her academics at Coastal Kzn College “Umlazi Tech” she then relocated to Johannesburg in 2013 in search of a job...in early 2016 “the Kwaito Pooh” got an opportunity from Changing lives production in partner with Vanande Holdings where she was casted in a Pan African movie called “Untainted” She portrayed a character of “Caroline. A young -vibrant, focused, Intelligent student which was her first experience on the television and she nailed the role...after that experience more roles were given to her, She also worked with “Mthakathi Production” and “Favour media”.Apparently she grew up dancing & singing in the community library hall where she was born Emlazi W section, under the choreographer “Bhuti Somthu” and she always loved kwaito “she said”. MMCandy is in a construction sector, she owns a construction company that has operating since 2015 and she is a founder of a charity organisation called “Royal Hadassah Doce” I am a woman of God, she also have her clothing line “Kwaito Pooh”.in the music sector MMCandy met the young blood “FaraWize”at Vanande Holdings and one of the 2members “KingFara“ discovered her talent, He thengroomed & purified her to be the gold she is today in music..., KingFara really pulled out that amazing, incredible talent she has. Her first single was Vosho Ft Farawize and she got Popular byher hot singletitled Bheka Ngenzani Ft KingFara & Tish The Kwaito Pooh has also worked with the legends “Bra Thizawho was known for the song “Chocolate” in the early 90s, Professor, Emza and Character.
