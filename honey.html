Mukaila Idowu Oyindamola

Honey Drop




Biography

Mukaila idowu oyindamola a.k.a "Honeydrop". given birth to on the 1st of January, 1996 . studying political science at OLABISI ONABANJO UNIVERSITY (OOU).she attended EPIC INTERNATIONAL SCHOOLS. she is from an extended family, the last born of the family. she developed interest in Music many years ago while in High school. But, she started professionally in 2011. she use to be a rapper at first, then her fans call me M.I.C. Later on, she Felt she could do better as a vocalist, singer song writer. So she switched to Singing. So, the M.I.C then is now HONEY DROP. she released her first track in 2012 titled IBEBEDI, which was produced by Tobs,and afterwards, she put out more materials.
